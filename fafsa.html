What is FAFSA?

FAFSA (Free Application for Federal Student Aid) is an application that allows students to apply for financial aid through the government. This is entirely free to fill out, and it provides undergraduate students with a plethora of methods to go about payinh for college.Some of the options provided by FAFSA include Grants, Loans, and Work-Study opportunities. The opportunities made avalible to you are determined by filling out an application that determines your financial need. Though it may seem that this application only applies to students of low income, all students are encouraged to fill out their information, as they may still be qualified to recieve various forms of financial aid



Why is the FAFSA important?

The FAFSA form is a key component of many the methods several programs, awards, and institutions use to determine if you qualify for any sort of financial aid. Often times, this even includes scholarship awards that prioritize financial need for their awards.
