The University of Texas at Permian Basin

Basic Info

The University of Texas Permian Basin is a public university in Odessa, Texas. It is part of the University of Texas System. UTPB was authorized by the Texas Legislature in 1969 and founded in 1973.

FINANCIAL AID: FASFA, Federal Work-Study, Fed Direct PLus Loan/Direct Loan, FSEOG, TCWSP, Hinson-Hazelwood (CAL) Loan, TEG, Academic and Athletic scholarships, Federal Pell Grant, Special scholarships
